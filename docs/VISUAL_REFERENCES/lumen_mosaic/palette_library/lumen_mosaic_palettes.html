05

Abyssal Bioluminescence

Mariana-trench dark base punctured by electric jewel glows — anglerfish lure, jellyfish violet, dinoflagellate cyan, pyrosome pink. The two near-black anchors are the trick: they let the bright cells read as emissive points rather than equal members of a chord. Closest the library gets to the “light through stained glass” reference image.

06

Kintsugi

Japanese pottery repaired with gold. Sumi-ink and indigo as the base, gold seam and saffron as the metallic accents, raku red and persimmon for warmth, verdigris and tea green for oxidised contrast. The most disciplined palette on offer — limited hue families, deep saturation. Closer to regal purples, browns, slates register you asked for at LM.4.3 than anything LM.4.5 attempted.
