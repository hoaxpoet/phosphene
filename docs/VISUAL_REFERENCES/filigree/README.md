## Preset summary

A physarum (slime-mold) agent network. The fine **searching web** is the song's
resting state; continuous energy brightens/quickens it; structural peaks **bloom**
it into consolidated gold **veins**. Palette: Kintsugi — gold veins on pure black.

---

## Reference images (interim)

### `01_web_home_searching.jpg` — the home state (MANDATORY)
The fine, dense, gold searching web on pure black. This is where the song spends
most of its time. **Trustable traits:** filament fineness, gold-on-pure-black
figure-ground, even coverage, organic (non-grid) tangle. **Disregard:** exact loop
positions (the web is always reorganising — it's a behaviour, not a fixed frame).

### `02_veins_payoff.jpg` — the energy payoff (MANDATORY)
Bold, bright, consolidated gold veins with **dark interiors**. This is the target
for a structural peak — the "load-bearing veins" read. **Trustable:** vein
thickness/brightness contrast, interiors stay dark. **Disregard:** exact cell
arrangement.

### `anti_01_bloom_overfill.jpg` — ANTI-REFERENCE (must NOT look like this)
The extreme-peak over-fill: figure-ground inverts to a bright gold field with dark
holes. The veins-on-black reading is lost. PHYS.4 caps the bloom so the payoff
stops at `02` and never reaches this. If a render looks like this, the bloom is
pushed too far.

## Rubric — Mandatory (for certification per V.6; checked at PHYS.5)

- [ ] **Web is the home state** — at low/moderate energy the field is the fine
  searching web (`01`), not consolidated veins.
- [ ] **Energy consolidates to veins** — a structural peak blooms bold bright veins
  with dark interiors (`02`); the form visibly tracks energy with the audio muted.
- [ ] **No bloom over-fill** — the payoff never inverts to bright-field-with-holes
  (`anti_01`).
- [ ] **The web is alive** — continuous reorganisation/search in motion, not a
  frozen texture (verify on a multi-frame sequence, not a still).
- [ ] **Audio-responsive through deviation primitives (D-026)** — energy envelope
  from the stem/full-mix blend; bloom as a threshold of it; collapse on a
  structural trigger (not beat phase).
- [ ] **Flash-safe** — collapse/bloom hold steady global luminance (no crater/spike;
  D-157). Measured in the multi-pass harness.
- [ ] **Graceful silence fallback** — at zero energy the web is dim but present and
  slowly evolving, never black/static.
- [ ] **Performance within tier budget** — proven ~0.66 ms/frame @ 1080p; not at risk.
- [ ] **Matt-approved live frame match** — live playback signed off against `01`/`02`.
